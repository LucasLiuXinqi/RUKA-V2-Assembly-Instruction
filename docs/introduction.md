# Introduction

## About RUKA-V2

RUKA-V2 is a robotic hand designed for research and development purposes. This assembly guide provides comprehensive instructions for building your own RUKA-V2 robotic hand.

## Features

- Multi-degree of freedom finger articulation
- Tendon-driven actuation system
- Integrated encoders for precise position feedback
- Modular design for easy maintenance

## Prerequisites

Before starting the assembly process, ensure you have:

1. Reviewed the complete [Bill of Materials](bom.md)
2. Gathered all necessary [tools](tools.md)
3. Allocated sufficient workspace
4. Basic understanding of mechanical assembly and electronics

## Safety Considerations

- Wear appropriate safety equipment when using tools
- Handle small parts carefully to avoid loss or injury
- Follow proper electrical safety procedures when working with electronics
- Keep workspace clean and organized

## Estimated Assembly Time

- **Mechanical Assembly**: 4-6 hours
- **Electronics Integration**: 2-3 hours
- **Calibration**: 1-2 hours
- **Total**: 7-11 hours

## Support

If you encounter issues during assembly, refer to the [Troubleshooting](troubleshooting.md) section or contact the RUKA-V2 community for support.
