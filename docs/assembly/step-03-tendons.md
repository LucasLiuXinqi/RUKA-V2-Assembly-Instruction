## Objective
Route and secure flexion and extension tendons through the fingers and palm.

## Parts
- Tendon string
- PTFE guide tubes

## Procedure
1. Insert PTFE tubes along designed tendon paths.
2. Thread tendons from distal to proximal.
3. Secure distal anchors with knots or clamps.

## Checkpoint
- Tendons slide smoothly
- Fingers return passively when released
