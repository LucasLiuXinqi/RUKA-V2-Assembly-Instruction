# Step 03 — Knuckle Assembly

### 1. Before Assembly

Things to check:

* The abduction / Adduction surface should be smooth enough
* Finger tendons are coming out from the correct place

### 3. After Assembly

Things to check:

* Try to push the abduction/adduction joint, see if it can move freely.&#x20;
* Check that the PTFE tubes do not exceed the wrist connector inner surface.
* Try to pull the strings and see if fingers can bend.



### 4. Troubleshooting

* Why my abduction/adduction joint stuck at one position?
  * Have you grind the joint contace surfaces?
  * Is the PTFE tube extruding out from the knuckle?
