## Objective
Assemble individual finger modules before tendon installation.

## Parts
- Finger links
- Pins or fasteners
- Joint bearings (if applicable)

## Procedure
1. Assemble finger joints in order from proximal to distal.
2. Verify full passive range of motion.
3. Do not install tendons yet.

## Checkpoint
- Each finger moves freely by hand
- No joint binding or excessive friction
